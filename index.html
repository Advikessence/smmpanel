Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our SMM services.

What is an SMM panel?

An SMM (Social Media Marketing) panel is a platform that provides various social media marketing services like followers, likes, views, and more to help grow your social media presence.

Is it safe to use NepalSMM services?

Yes, all our services are safe and comply with the terms of service of social media platforms. We don't require passwords or sensitive information.

How quickly will I see results?

Most orders start processing within minutes of placement. Depending on the service, complete delivery can take anywhere from a few minutes to 24 hours.

Do you accept Nepali Rupees (NPR)?

Yes, we accept payments in NPR through various local payment methods including eSewa, Khalti, ConnectIPS, IME Pay, and bank transfers.

Which cryptocurrencies do you accept?

We accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT, and Litecoin (LTC) as payment methods.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer refunds for orders that are not delivered within the specified time frame or if the quality doesn't meet our standards.

Do I need to share my account password?

No, we never ask for your account passwords. We only need the public link to your profile or post.

Can I become a reseller?

Yes, we offer reseller packages with special discounts and API access for seamless integration with your own platform.
